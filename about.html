What are your goals?

Our team focuses on many aspects of our subject; in this section, you can see our content focus, our objectives, what we do, and a summary of the content itself.

Educational

Although our dangerous topic includes such violence, we would like for our podcast to be educational in order to help them out during difficult times on their own.

Entertaining

One of our main goals throughout the podcast is to keep the viewers interested and entertained so that they will listen in. We'd do stunts, jokes, and other things to keep them entertained while they learned about our topic.

Instructive

These instructions are one of our main points in the stream, assisting teenagers in particular who are in need by teaching them about things and assisting them in avoiding danger.

Informative

TWith all of these factors combined, an informative stream would help many people learn more about this issue and spread the word so that other people their age or similar would not suffer harm or anything of the sort.
